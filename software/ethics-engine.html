What It Is

A psychometric evaluation platform that profiles AI language models to detect biases, ethical risks, and behavioral patterns before deployment. Just as personality tests reveal human tendencies, the Ethics Engine reveals AI tendencies across dimensions like political bias, cultural sensitivity, risk aversion, and decision-making patterns. Organizations use it to validate AI safety, meet regulatory requirements, and build trust with stakeholders.
