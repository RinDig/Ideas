What It Is

A mobile-first AI assistant designed for construction supervisors and field crews. Workers ask questions like "What paint spec for Building 3?" or "Order 50 bags of concrete mix" and get instant answers pulled from project documents, specifications, and supplier integrations. Eliminates phone calls, reduces errors, speeds up decision-making in the field. Built for the hands-on reality of construction where time is money and mistakes are expensive.
